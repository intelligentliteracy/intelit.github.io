Functional illiteracy is defined as reading and writing skills that are inadquate to manage everyday situations and employment tasks that require literary skills beyond a basic level

Our mission is to create a fun and easy way for the functionally illiterate to learn and enjoy the experience of reading and comprehension


85% of juveniles who interact with the juvenile court system are functionally illiterate

45 million people are functionally illiterate and read below a fifth grade level
